Teams and matchups

Teams and matches are randomly formed by tournament organizer (you can verify the algorithm from source code). Players are not allowed to switch teams. It is possible to get same team mate or even same opposing more then once.

How do I know when my next game is?

You need to contact your team mate and the oppenent team to agree when you will play. Go to games page and see who are your team mates.

How do I report the result of a game?

You report the result by using this web app. Go to games page and find the game. Insert both teams' scores by using the dropdowns, but be careful, you can only report scores once.

What if I accidentally report wrong score or for wrong game?

Just dont! If that really happens, contact system administrator (Mikko).

Will there be a prize for the winner?

Definitely!
