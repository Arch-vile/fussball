Teams and matchups

Teams and matches are randomly formed by tournament organizer (you can verify the algorithm from source code). Players are not allowed to switch teams. It is possible to get same team mate or even same opposing more than once.

How do I know when my next game is?

You need to contact your team mate and the opponent team to agree when you will play. There is no rush, just play when it best suits you. Go to games page and see who are your team mates.

Tournament will continue until all games are played. Lets try our best to complete the tournament in a month or so.

How do I report the result of a game?

You report the result by using this web app. Go to games page and find the game. Insert both teams' scores by using the drop downs and click "submit".

What if I accidentally report wrong score or for wrong game?

Just don't! If that really happens, contact system administrator (Mikko).

Will there be a prize for the winner?

Definitely!
